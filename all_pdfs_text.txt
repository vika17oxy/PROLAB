Understanding Kalman Filter: Measurement
• The Kalman Gain determines how much we trust:
• the prediction (model)
• the measurement (sensor)
• Interpretation
• Large 𝐾𝑡→ trust measurement more
• Small 𝐾𝑡→ trust prediction more
• 𝐾𝑡is computed automatically based on uncertainty:
• high measurement noise → small 𝐾𝑡 
• high prediction uncertainty → large 𝐾𝑡
