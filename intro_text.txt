• Deterministic models are insufficient when dealing with real-world uncertainties.
• Probabilistic methods allow for a more robust and adaptable approach to state 
estimation and control.
• Why Probabilistic Models?:
• They allow robots to handle sensor inaccuracies, actuator uncertainties, and 
unpredictable environments.

Improved Accuracy:
By accounting for noise and errors, probabilistic methods offer more 
accurate state estimations than deterministic methods.

Particle Filters) for localization and obstacle detection.

Basic Math: Reasoning Over Time
Using Bayes’ rule to answer the localization question:
